AI Compliance

Fraud Signal Intelligence Platform

Problem

Fraud and abuse signals were fragmented across teams, making escalation slow and high-risk events hard to triage.

Solution

I led the design of a monitoring layer that unified event streams, surfaced high-confidence anomalies, and connected operations to action.

My role

Product lead and systems architect, aligning analytics, engineering, and policy teams around a shared operating model.

Impact

Reduced false positives, improved review cycle time, and created a more repeatable control framework for high-sensitivity events.

Problem

My role

Product owner translating policy needs into an operational system that teams could actually use under pressure.

Impact

Improved review traceability, lowered operational friction, and made audit readiness much more reliable.
